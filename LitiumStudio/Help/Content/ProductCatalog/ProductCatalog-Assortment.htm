Assortments, product groups and products

Assortment

All assortments are displayed in the left menu along with the product group structure. A assortment (d) can exist in several languages and contain a structure of product groups (e) and products.

 

 

 

Create a assortment

It exists two ways of creating a new assortment.

  1. The first one is to right click on the node “Product catalog” in the left menu and select “New assortment” from the menu. The other one is to select “Product catalog” in the left menu and select “New” in the toolbar and click “Assortment” from the drop down menu.

 

The first step of “Create assortment” guide consists of option on what languages to use and also define the language dependent names.

 

Click “Save” to save the new assortment.

Click “Cancel” to abort changes made.

Edit a assortment

It exists two ways of editing a assortment.

  1. The first one is to right click on the specific assortment node and click “Edit”. The other option is to select the specific assortment node and select “Edit” from the toolbar.

 

The “Edit assortment” guide consists of options on what languages to use and also define the language dependent names.

 

Click “Save” to save the assortment.

Click “Cancel” to abort changes made.

Delete a assortment

It exists two ways of deleting a assortment.

  1. The first one is to right click on the specific assortment node and click “Delete”. The other option is to select the specific assortment node and select “Delete” from the toolbar.

Manage product groups

 

 

  1. Create a new product group under the selected product group or assortment.
  2. Edit the selected product group.
  3. Delete the selected product group.
  4. Cut the selected product group.
  5. Copy the selected product group.
  6. Paste a product group under the new selection.
  7. Move the selected product group into a new postion.
  8. Publish all products that exists within the product group
  9. Unpublish all products that exists within the product group
  10. Assign or unassign products of the selected product group to price agents.
  11. Export the selected product group into XML.

 

Create product group

It exists two ways of creating a new product group.

  1. The first one is to right click on the assortment or product group node which the new product group should be created under. Now select “New product group” (a) from the menu. The other one is to select the assortment or product group node, which the new product group should be created under, and select “New” in the toolbar and click “Product group” from the drop down menu.

“Create new product group” guide is displayed in two steps.

Create product group - Step 1

The first step of “Create product groups” guide is to decide what field template to use for this product group. The product group will be created in those languages that this assortmen exists in.

 

Create product group - Step 2

The first section of information contains general information for the product group. The fields displayed are:

  • Display name - The field that are marked as “Display name” in current field template will be shown here. In this particular case, it is called “Name” and will be the same for all languages. However, this is a field setting that is dependent on your field template.
  • URL name - Add the URL name for a specific language. The full URL depends on where in a assortment the article is published.

 

Other sections of information depends on the configuration of current field template. All fields that are grouped (See settings for current field template) together will be displayed in a information area (See grouped information area in picture below) and fields that are not grouped together will all be displayed in a separate area (See common information area in picture below).

 

 

 

All required fields needs to be filled in before saving the product group and they marked with a blue icon.

 

Click “Save” to save the new product group.

Click “Cancel” to abort changes made.

Other sections of information depends on the configuration of current field template. Delete product group

To delete a product group, right click on that product group and click “Delete” (c).

All products and product groups that exists underneath will be deleted.

Working with product group relations

To create a relation between a product group and a product/product group, right click on the product group and select “Relations” (h). All relation types that are connected to the current field template can be used. See the settings for the current field template for more information.

 

A product group can be associated with products and product groups, depending on the implementation of the layout.

 

 

To create a relation to a product or product group:

  1. Select which “Relation type” that should be used by selecting in from the drop down list.
  2. Select the “Add” link next to the “Product” or “Product group” panel, depending if the relation should be associated with a product or product group.
  3. Fromthe popup-window, select one or several items and click “Save”. They will now be shown in the panel.

 

To remote a relation to a product or produt group:

  1. Locate the product or product group to which the relation should be deleted.
  2. Select the delete button, to the right.
  3. The relation will bow be deleted.
Export product group structure

A product structure can be exported into a XML-document, that can be used to import information into another system for example Adobe InDesign.

 

To export a product structure:

  1. Right click on that product group from which the export should begin.
  2. Select “Export” (k) and a export guide will be shown.

 

It exists several settings when exporting product information.

  • Language - Select in which language the language dependent information should be shown. It is only possible to select one language at a time.
  • Include sub product groups - If the export only should export current product group or its sub groups.
  • Transform output - The standard XML-file are structured in a standard way. If it exists a demand for another structure, it is possible to transform the output using XSL. See Litium Studio Knowledge Center for more information.
  • Save images and documents to disc - To which folder should the images and documents that belongs to the exported product structure be saved. The file path is located on the server where Litium Studio is installed. See Litium Studio Knowledge Center for more information about what permissions that is needed to save images and documents.
  • Choose how images and documents will be exported:
    Path to the webserver for access thru Litium Studio - A URL will be provided in the XML file to a downloadable version of images and documents.
    As binary data directly in the XML-file - The binary data will be written to the XML-file. This will create a large data amount.

 

Click “Export” to export the XML-file.

Click “Cancel” to abort changes made.

Edit a product

A product itself can’t be edited, it is article or variant group that will be edited.

  1. Select the product group to which the product are connected. A product grid will be displayed to the right.
  2. In the product grid, select the product that should be edited.
  3. Click “Edit” (b) found in the toolbar.
  4. A edit guide will now be shown. What guide depends on if it is a variant group or article that is edited. See “Edit article” or “Edit variant group” for more information.
Delete a product

To delete a product.

  1. Select the product group to which the product are connected. A product grid will be displayed to the right.
  2. In the product grid, select the product that should be deleted.
  3. Click “Delete” (c) found in the toolbar. The product will be deleted from the product group, but not the article itself.
Move products

It exists two ways in how to move a product from one product group into another.

  • To cut and paste a product, select the product and click “Cut”. Now select the product group that the product should be stored in and click “Paste”.
  • It is also possible to drag-and-drop a product. Select the product and drag it into a new product group in the left menu. The product will now be moved.
Publish products and product groups

A product will not be displayed on the web until it is published.

  1. Select the product group to which the product are connected. A product grid will be displayed to the right.
  2. In the product grid, select the product that should be published.
  3. Click “Publish” (e) found in the toolbar. A drop down menu will be shown with several options.

 

 

  • Publish selected product - Only selected products will be affected.
  • Publish product group - The product group will be published, but not the products.
  • Publish product group and content - Everything inside this product group will be published.
  • Unpublish selected products - Only selected products will be affected.
  • Unpublish product group and content - Everything inside this product group will be unpublished.
Assign products to price agents

With Litium Studio, it is possible to publish product information to price agents.

 

  1. Select the product group to which the product are connected. A product grid will be displayed to the right.
  2. In the product grid, select the product that should be assigned to price agent.
  3. Click “Price agents” (f) found in the toolbar. A drop down menu will be shown with several options.

 

 

  • Add all - All products in current product group will be assigned to price agent list.
  • All selected products to price agents - All selected products will be assigned to price agent list.
  • Remove all from price agent list - All products in current product group will be removed from price agent list.
  • Remove selected item from price agents - All selected products will be removed from price agent list.

 

For more information about what price agents Litium Studio supports and how to use it, please visit Litium Studio Knowledge Center.

Edit sort order

It is possible to define the sort order or products on the web site.

  1. Select the product group in which you want to define the sort order.
  2. In the toolbar, select “Edit sort order” (h).

A grid, containg all products in current product group, will be shown. From here it is possible to sort the products into prefered order, by selecting columns or drag and drop rows.

 

Click “Save” when done with sort order.

Click “Cancel” to abort changes made.
