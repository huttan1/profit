Payments

All payments are shown in the payment list, found when selecting “Payments” in the left menu. It is also possible to select a range of payments by using a predefined filter, “Today”, “Yesterday, “This week”, “This month”, “This year” and “All.

Manage payments

 

 

  1. View details about selected payment.
  2. Edit selected payment. If it is enabled depends on payment status settings.
  3. Delete selected payment. If it is enabled depends on payment status settings.
  4. Reserve selected payment.
  5. Charge selected payment.
  6. Cancel payment of selected.
  7. Return payment. Might be possible depending on selected provider.
  8. View related order or delivery associated with selected payment.
  9. Filter payment list based on a specified status.
  10. Filter payment list based on payment method of selected payment provider.
  11. Filter payment list based on a specified payment provider.
  12. Filter payment list based on a specified start date.
  13. Filter payment list based on a specified end date.
View payment
  1. Select a payment (1) from the list.
  2. Click “View” (a).

Click “Back” to return to the payment list.

Edit payment

Depending on payment provider and in what status this payment occur, it might not be possible to edit a payment.

  1. Select a payment (1) from the list.
  2. Click “Edit” (b) the “Edit payment” popup will be shown.
  3. Specify what payment provider and payment method to use.

 

Click “Update”. Payment info will be updated accordingly.

Delete payment

Depending on payment provider and in what status this payment occur, it might not be possible to delete a payment.

  1. Select a payment (1) from the list.
  2. Click “Delete” (c).
Reserve

Payment providers treats the reserve functionality different. In general, reserve money means that money are reserved from customer account but it is not charged yet.

 

  1. Select the payment which you want to reserve from the payment list.
  2. Click “Reserve” (d).
  3. Depending on choice of payment provider, different action might occur.
Charge

Payment providers treats the charge functionality different. In general, charge money means that money are charged from customer account.

  1. Select the payment (1) which you want to reserve from the payment list.
  2. Click “Charge” (e).
  3. The charge request will be handled depending on the order payment provider and its payment status.
Cancel payment

Depending on payment provider and in what status this payment occur, it might not be possible to cancel a payment.

  1. Select a payment (1) from the list.
  2. Click “Cancel payment” (f).
Return payment

Depending on payment provider and in what status this payment occur, it might not be possible to return a payment that are already charged.

  1. Select a payment (1) from the list.
  2. Click “Return payment” (g).
View related orders and deliveries
  1. Select the paymen (1) which you require to view related order or delivery details.
  2. Click “View related” (h).

Select deliveries or payments from the drop down list. You will then be redirected to either payments or deliveries associated with the order.
